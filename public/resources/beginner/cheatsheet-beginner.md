# Beginner VA Cheat Sheet

## Finding Your Niche
- List your existing skills (admin, writing, social media, design, etc.)
- Research what clients are hiring for on Upwork, OnlineJobs.ph
- Pick a niche that matches your skills AND has demand
- Start general, then specialize as you gain experience

## Setting Up Services
- Offer 3 packages: Basic, Standard, Premium
- Price based on value, not hours
- Get testimonials from your first clients
- Review and raise rates every 6 months

## Profile Tips
- Clear, professional headline
- Showcase your best work
- Include client testimonials
- Keep it updated
