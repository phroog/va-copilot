# VA Agency Launch Guide

## Step 1: Solidify Your Foundation
- Have at least 3 active clients
- Generate $3k+/month consistently
- Have clear SOPs for every service

## Step 2: Find Your First Hire
- Look for VAs with complementary skills
- Start with one sub-VA on a trial basis
- Create a simple training manual

## Step 3: Transition Clients
- Introduce your team gradually
- Maintain quality control
- Keep communication transparent

## Step 4: Scale the Team
- Add VAs as client demand grows
- Hire a project manager when you hit 5+ VAs
- Focus on sales and systems, not delivery

## Step 5: Systemize Everything
- Standard operating procedures for all tasks
- Regular team meetings and check-ins
- Performance metrics and reviews
- Client feedback loops
