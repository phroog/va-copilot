# Scaling Your VA Business - Checklist

## Phase 1: Prepare to Scale
- [ ] Systematize all your processes (SOPs)
- [ ] Create templates for common tasks
- [ ] Build a client onboarding流程
- [ ] Set up financial tracking

## Phase 2: Raise Your Rates
- [ ] Analyze your current pricing
- [ ] Research market rates for your niche
- [ ] Communicate rate increases professionally
- [ ] Replace low-paying clients

## Phase 3: Build Your Brand
- [ ] Optimize LinkedIn profile
- [ ] Collect and display testimonials
- [ ] Start a blog or newsletter
- [ ] Network with other VAs

## Phase 4: Expand
- [ ] Identify tasks you can delegate
- [ ] Find and vet sub-VAs
- [ ] Create training materials
- [ ] Set up quality control processes
